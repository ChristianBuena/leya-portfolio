About Me

I'm a freelance web developer and UI designer based in Bangkok, Thailand, specializing in building responsive, user-focused websites that combine clean design with strong functionality.

With a background in both front-end and back-end development, I create full-stack solutions—from pixel-perfect interfaces to API integration and custom CMS features.

I have a strong passion for SEO optimization, clean code, and creating seamless user experiences that drive engagement and growth. Whether it's a blog, landing page, or full web app, I focus on building with clarity, speed, and adaptability.

My Journey

From curiosity to code, my journey into tech was never just about websites—it's about creating experiences that connect people.

I started as someone who loved visuals: color palettes, clean layouts, and the way a good design just feels right. But as I explored more, I realized that design alone wasn't enough—I wanted to build the logic behind it too.

Over the years, I've built responsive blogs, landing pages with real conversion impact, and full custom CMS-powered sites that clients can manage on their own.

Every project taught me something—whether it was optimizing performance, debugging an impossible issue, or understanding how users actually interact with a page.

Today, I combine design, code, and strategy to create digital spaces that are both functional and beautiful. The journey's still ongoing, and I'm excited for what comes next.
